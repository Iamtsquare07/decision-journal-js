Decision Journal

Track your decisions with the James Clear' Atomic Habits Decision journal. Record your major decisions to review them each week, month or year to know what you got right or wrong. Track your best decisions to replicate them in the future, and please , try not to judge yourself in the process, simply act as an observer.
